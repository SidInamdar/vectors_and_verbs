Semantic Alchemists: SkipGram and Continuous Bag Of Words (CBOW)

Lnaguage models require vector representations of words to capture semantic relationships. Earlier than 2010s, models used word count based vector representations that captured only the frequency of words. For Example, one-hot encoding. The best part was that it could create much denser relationships between words than prior models and it was unsupervised.
